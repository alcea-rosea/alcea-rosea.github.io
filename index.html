Hollyhock Quick Reference

Alcea Rosea is a fan-made quick reference website for Nobilis and Chuubo's Marvelous Wish-Granting Engine, two excellent role-playing games by Jenna Moran that facilitate telling collaborative stories with a focus on player agency and the interplay between the mythic and the personal.

{% include not_standalone.html %}

Nobilis

Mythic-scale adventures of the demi-gods tasked with upholding the conceptual underpinnings of reality itself.

Chuubo's Marvelous Wish-Granting Engine

Personal and emotional narratives of the miraculous and the mundane in a strange world ravaged by the bleak forces of the Outside.
